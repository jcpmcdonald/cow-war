Cow War

COW POWER! Make your cows work for you by sending them to the mill where they will increase your cow power. The more cow power you have, the more Cattleium your farm will have. Spend Cattleium on more cows! Alternatively, send your cows to their death. The first side to successfully hit the opponent's barn 5 times wins.

Created for the GDSE Fall 2014 Game Jam. The theme was a "Potluck": submit 5 words, receive 5 random words from those submitted, use 3 for your personal theme. I was assigned the words: Wind, Power, Gears, Cows, BBQ, making use of the bold words. All assets were created myself, thank goodness I found a good cow pic to trace.
